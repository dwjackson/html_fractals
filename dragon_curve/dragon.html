Dragon Curve

This page draws dragon curves. That should be fairly obvious based on the header. You can define the number of iterations for the curve below. Also, you can save the curve as an image if you want to. Just right click and save it like you would any other image.

Note: The more iterations you do, the longer the dragon curve will take to generate. For example, just 11 iterations takes several second to generate. You really don't want to go more than 12 iterations, by the way, or you'll be here a while.
